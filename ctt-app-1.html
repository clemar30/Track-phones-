Recovery Incentives

Reward & Recovery Centre

Post financial rewards for device recovery, mark recovered devices, and share stories that help the community protect itself better.

💰 Reward Details
ℹ How Rewards Work

1. Post your reward — Enter your case ID and the amount you are offering for information or return of your device.

2. Visible to community — Your reward appears in the Active Rewards feed for all CTT users to see.

3. Finder contacts you — Via your preferred contact method through CTT's secure messaging.

4. Mark as recovered — Once your device is returned, update the case status.

5. Share your story — Help others by sharing how recovery happened.
